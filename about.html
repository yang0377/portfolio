About

As a Photographer

I focus on documentary and feminist-style imagery. With a strong emphasis on minority individuals and women, my work aims to capture their true essence and challenge the conventional male gaze.

As a Designer

Admiring wabi-sabi (侘寂) aesthetics, I cherish the beauty of simplicity, creating space where imagination can flow freely while embracing imperfections. My designs aim to evoke tranquility, contemplation, and a profound appreciation for the inherent uniqueness of each creation.
